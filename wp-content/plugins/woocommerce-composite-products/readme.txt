Create configurable product kits of any size or complexity.

== Description ==

Pick products from your catalog and create configurable product kits of any size or complexity. With support for simple and variable products, downloadable products and product bundles, Composite Products is a powerful extension that works great in a wide range of kitting applications with strict inventory management requirements.

Read the full documentation [here](http://docs.woocommerce.com/document/composite-products/).
